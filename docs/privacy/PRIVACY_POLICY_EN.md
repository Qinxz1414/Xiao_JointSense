# Privacy Policy

**JointSense Privacy Policy**

Effective Date: April 16, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

JointSense ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our application.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully before using the App. If you do not agree with any part of this Privacy Policy, please stop using the App immediately.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Test Data**: Test item names and session information you input when performing inflammatory factor tests
- **Feedback**: Feedback and suggestions you voluntarily provide to us

### 2.2 Images You Capture/Select
- **Reaction Chamber Images**: Images of chip reaction chambers you capture or select from your gallery
- The App does not automatically capture or upload any other images from your device

### 2.3 Device Information
- **Camera Permission**: Required to capture chip reaction chamber images
- **Storage Permission**: Used to save and read image files you select

### 2.4 Test Result Data
- Inflammatory factor test results (IL-6, TNF-α, IL-1β concentration values)
- RGB color analysis data
- Test timestamps

---

## 3. How We Use Information

### 3.1 Image Processing
- Images you capture or select are processed only on your local device
- Image data is never uploaded to any servers or third-party cloud services

### 3.2 Detection Analysis
- Local algorithms are used to analyze inflammatory factor concentrations from images
- Detection reports containing RGB color values and concentration data are generated

### 3.3 Data Storage
- All test results and history are stored only locally on your device
- You can view, export, or delete this data at any time

### 3.4 Data Backup
- The App uses Android Auto Backup feature
- Backup data is stored in your Google Cloud account, including only app settings and preferences
- Backup does NOT include your detection images and result data

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

This App does **NOT** contain any third-party advertisements, analytics, or tracking services.

---

## 5. Data Security

We implement the following security measures to protect your data:
- All data processing is performed locally on your device
- Industry-standard encryption measures are used to protect data transmission
- Regular app updates to fix security vulnerabilities

## 6. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

### 6.1 Right of Access
- You can view your test history at any time within the App

### 6.2 Right of Deletion
- You can delete detection data and history from your device at any time
- Uninstalling the App will automatically delete all local data on the device

### 6.3 Data Export
- You can view detailed detection result data
